**PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS:**
*   **Admin Dashboard:**
    *   Accessible via a special short link (no password).
    *   Add/Edit/Delete students (Name, Mobile, Photo).
    *   A feature to select a Player of the League (نجم الدوري).
    *   Create competitions: Admin adds a question, multiple-choice options, marks the correct answer, and assigns points.
    *   Manage a football league table for four teams.
    *   A button to print all student QR codes at once.
*   **Student View:**
    *   Each student has a personal page accessible via a unique QR code.
    *   The page displays their info, total points, and a log of points transactions.
    *   It shows active competitions for the student to answer.
    *   It displays a Top 10 leaderboard and the football league standings.
*   **Points System:**
    *   Admins can add/deduct points for each student based on a predefined list of positive and negative actions (e.g., Early attendance: +10, Absence: -60). Each reason should have an associated emoji.
*   **Team System:**
    *   Students are assigned to one of four teams: الصدارة (Leadership), النخبة (Elite), الزعامة (Mastery), الريادة (Pioneering).
    *   Each team has a distinct color/frame for its members in the UI.
*   **Branding:**
    *   The club logo should be used as an icon and a faint background.
    *   A custom footer should display تطوير أبوغيث (Developed by AbuGhaith).
*   **Spectator View:**
    *   A read-only link for another supervisor to view all data (leaderboards, competitions, league table) without editing capabilities.
